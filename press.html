Boilerplate

Baseline is a health app for iPhone that combines mobility, strength, and breathwork in a single app. Each session type takes under 20 minutes. The system is built around the concept of a minimum effective dose: the smallest amount of exercise that still produces a meaningful result, informed by peer-reviewed research. There is nothing to choose, plan, or configure. The system builds your session and you press start. Baseline costs $9.95 USD per year, compared to the $10 or more per month that most fitness apps charge. It requires no account, no internet connection, and no equipment. Baseline launches in May 2026.
