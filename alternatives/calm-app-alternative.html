FAQ

Calm vs Baseline.

Is Baseline a good alternative to Calm?

If you are overwhelmed by Calm's library of over a thousand sessions, Baseline is a simpler alternative. It offers one chosen session per day, no category scrolling, and includes daily movement alongside breathwork.

What is the difference between Calm and Baseline?

Calm is a meditation and sleep library with many sessions to choose from. Baseline is a daily movement and breathwork app with one session per day, already chosen. Calm focuses on mindfulness, Baseline focuses on daily physical habit.

Is there a cheaper alternative to Calm?

Yes. Baseline is $9.99/yr compared to Calm at approximately ~$70/yr. Baseline also includes daily movement and mobility, not just breathwork.
