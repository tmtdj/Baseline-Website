Override

Override uses deep, rhythmic breathing to shift your nervous system out of overwhelm. Forty breaths flood your system with oxygen before a sustained hold lets your body reset.

Minimum Effective Dose
2:40
40 breaths at 4s each. Inhale 2.5s, exhale 1.5s.

Breath pattern

2.5s in→1.5s out× 40

2.5 seconds in, 1.5 seconds out × 40 breaths

When to use it

Use when you're overwhelmed, panicking, or need to take back control right now.

The science

Cyclic hyperventilation (40 controlled breaths at a fast cadence) temporarily changes blood chemistry, reducing CO₂ and increasing blood pH. This produces tingling, lightheadedness, and a powerful shift in autonomic state. The subsequent breath hold leverages the dive reflex, triggering a vagal brake that brings heart rate down sharply.
