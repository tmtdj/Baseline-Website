Reset

Reset uses a physiological sigh, a double inhale followed by a long exhale. Research shows cyclic sighing is more effective than meditation for reducing physiological stress.

Minimum Effective Dose
1 min
4 cycles at 14s per breath. Inhale 4s, sip, exhale 8s.

Breath pattern

4s in→sip→8s out

4 seconds in, sip, 8 seconds out

When to use it

Use after a stressful meeting, a difficult conversation, or when you feel wrung out.

The science

The physiological sigh (double inhale + extended exhale) was identified by Stanford researchers (Balban et al., 2023) as more effective than meditation for reducing physiological stress. The sip re-inflates collapsed alveoli in the lungs, maximising CO₂ offloading on the long exhale. In the study, 5 minutes of daily cyclic sighing reduced respiratory rate and improved mood within the first session.
