Supine Lumbar Rotations

Lumbar spine mobility, lower back relief, spinal decompression

Minimum Effective Dose
40s
The shortest time that still creates real, measurable change. Hit the dose and the benefit starts. Everything after is bonus.

Why it works

Gentle rotations in a supine position allow the lower back to decompress without load. Gravity does the work — each exhale is permission to go a little further, not a demand.

How to do it

Lower ribs melt down.
Let gravity rotate you. Breathe out and soften, don't force range.
  • Lie on your back with knees bent
  • Let both knees fall to one side
  • Breathe out and let gravity deepen the rotation
  • Switch sides slowly — no forcing
