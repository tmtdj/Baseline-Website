Standing Weight-Shift Rocks

Proprioception, weight distribution awareness, balance foundations

Minimum Effective Dose
1 min
The shortest time that still creates real, measurable change. Hit the dose and the benefit starts. Everything after is bonus.

Why it works

Weight-shift rocks build awareness of how you distribute force through your feet. Most people live entirely on their toes or heels without knowing it. This exercise makes the pattern visible.

How to do it

Rock forward and back over your feet.
Feel your weight shift from toes to heels. Slow, controlled, grounded. Notice where your weight naturally settles. That tells you something.
  • Stand with feet hip-width apart, arms relaxed
  • Slowly shift your weight forward onto your toes, then back onto your heels
  • Feel where your weight naturally settles and keep the movement slow and controlled
  • Notice where your weight naturally settles — that tells you something
