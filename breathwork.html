The research

5 minutes of breathwork
outperformed mindfulness meditation.

A 2023 Stanford study compared daily 5-minute breathwork to daily 5-minute mindfulness meditation across one month. The breathwork group, specifically cyclic sighing, showed greater improvements in mood, anxiety reduction, and resting respiratory rate than the meditation group.
